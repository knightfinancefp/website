Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions about my services.

What information do I need to provide?

Typically, I’ll require your profit and loss report, balance sheet, and any supporting financial information available from your accounting software.

Do I need accounting software?

No. While reports from software such as Xero, QuickBooks, or Sage are helpful, I can work with spreadsheets and other financial records where appropriate.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Most reports are completed within 3–5 working days of receiving the required information. More detailed engagements may take longer depending on complexity.

Do you offer tax or financial advice services?

No. I purely provide you with a clear representation of your performance. I present my findings and identify areas of improvement.

Can you help improve profitability?

Yes. My analysis focuses on identifying trends, cost drivers, performance issues, and opportunities to improve financial results.

Is there a long-term commitment?

No. You can purchase a one-off package or discuss ongoing support depending on your business needs.
